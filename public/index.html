STARSCRAP

Survive, scavenge, and uncover impostors in a chaotic 2D battle royale!

This game is a 2D battle royale game.
The main goal is to be the last one standing and win your team.

You can only kill if you have a weapon.

You can get weapon by scavenge items in the map.

You can also kill other players to get their weapon.

The last one standing will be the winner.
